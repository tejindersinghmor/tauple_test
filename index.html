Blockchain as a Service Platform

Blockchains are an emerging technology pattern that can radically improve banking, supply-chain and other transaction networks, giving them new opportunities for innovation and growth while reducing cost and risk.

With blockchain businesses can significantly reduce cost on networking and validation. Businesses can now have transparency inside and outside the organisation and single point of truth between mulitple parties or systems with immutable data records.

With Tauple Baas we provide a plug and play system to develop, deploy and scale blockchain applications with ease. Tauple provides complete solution for enterprises with interoperabilty and enterprise ready api's

The first part of deployement is done on hyperledger fabric and ethereum private network. With quick movement towards adopting smart contract platform like Tezos and cosmos hub for cross chain transactions.

Open scalable blockchain infrastructure
×

Open Consensus for billions

Creating a blockchain that can support the future of billions with high throughput and low latency. Tauple public blockchain is an open source project giving resources to the world to build blockchain applications with this consensus method and drive development of the protocol.

We understand the scalability problem with blockchain infrstructure right now and how different off chain and on chain solutions are being developed to suffuce this problem. With Tauple we are building infrastructure that enables sharding with cross sharding, smart contract sharding, atomic swaps and new incentive modelsolving the scalability problem with better functionality and level of trust.
